Why I love Excel and Sheets

While the softwares are not coding/programming related, Excel and Gsuite's Spreadheets have very powerful functions that enable you to make incredible visualisations, dashboards and insights.

While the tech-field is growing at an exponential rate and there is an ever-incsreasing demand for analysts, it's important to remember movements like the "No-code" movements that champion efforts, apps, and cultures that strive to ensure that people can still make their analyses and build softwares and tools without having any programming skills. The spreadsheet programmes will always be relevant due to their ability to produce detailed reports without the knowledge of code.
