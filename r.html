R

Robust.

Commonly praised for its diverse array of statistical packages and strong focus on statistical analysis.

Why I love R

This was the first language I learned when I started learning about analytics. I remember spending hours trying to understand concepts when I was learning this in the Google Data Analytics course. 7 days later, I was done with the entire course and ready to start my case study.

R has a LARGE pool of packages you can choose from for your projects. This can seem overwhelming at first, but something I absolutely love about R is the abundance of documentation. For every package, function, or feature you are guaranteed to be able to find a write-up or two about it. My tool of choice with using R is RStudio. I love the interface because I can have my code, output, reference files and documentation visible on the same page. One of my absolutely favourite packages are `tidyverse` and `data.table`.
